Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Developer: Salim Shaikh  ·  Contact: statsguysalim@gmail.com

What we collect

Heart Rate Monitor records a short video of your face solely to estimate your heart rate using remote photoplethysmography (rPPG). The video is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our processing server, analysed, and immediately deleted. We do not store your video, any facial data, or any biometric identifiers.

Heart rate readings

Estimated BPM readings are stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. They are never uploaded, shared, or sold. You can delete all readings at any time from the History tab inside the app.

Camera & microphone

Camera permission is required to record the face video. Microphone permission is required by the Android/iOS operating system for video recording; audio is never processed, transmitted, or stored.

No third-party analytics

This app contains no advertising SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no analytics libraries. It does not share any data with third parties.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
